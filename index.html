Research Focus

I am a PhD Candidate at the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi, developing Digital Twins and Cyber-Physical Systems for bioprocessing applications. My research bridges advanced process control, soft sensors, and AI/ML to advance biopharmaceutical manufacturing.

  • Advanced Process Control — Designing controllers for complex bioprocesses
  • Soft Sensors — Real-time monitoring using data-driven approaches
  • AI/ML Applications — Leveraging machine learning for process optimization
  • Digital Transformation — Implementing Industry 4.0 in biomanufacturing
