# About Me

I am a PhD Candidate at the **Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi** (IIT Delhi), pursuing research in Digital Twins and Cyber-Physical Systems for bioprocessing. My work combines process engineering, control systems, and artificial intelligence to advance biopharmaceutical manufacturing.

## Research Interests

- **Digital Twins & CPS:** Developing virtual representations of bioprocesses for real-time monitoring and control
- **Soft Sensors & PAT:** Designing data-driven sensors for process analytical technology
- **Machine Learning in Bioprocessing:** Applying AI/ML for prediction, optimization, and quality monitoring
- **Biomanufacturing:** Focus on continuous manufacturing of monoclonal antibodies and downstream processing
- **Process Control:** Advanced control strategies for complex bioprocesses

## Education

**PhD in Biochemical Engineering**, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi)

## Professional Experience

Over the course of my PhD, I have developed expertise in:

- Process simulation and modeling (CADET, SciPy, SymPy)
- Machine learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, scikit-learn)
- Cloud platforms (AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure)
- Real-time systems and embedded control
