<h2>Research Focus</h2>

<div class="about-content">
  <p>
    I am a PhD Candidate at the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi, where I focus on developing and implementing Digital Twins and Cyber-Physical Systems for bioprocessing applications. My research combines:
  </p>
  <ul style="margin-left: 1.5rem; margin-bottom: 1rem;">
    <li><strong>Advanced Process Control:</strong> Designing controllers for complex bioprocesses</li>
    <li><strong>Soft Sensors:</strong> Real-time monitoring using data-driven approaches</li>
    <li><strong>AI/ML Applications:</strong> Leveraging machine learning for process optimization and prediction</li>
    <li><strong>Digital Transformation:</strong> Implementing Industry 4.0 principles in biomanufacturing</li>
  </ul>
  <p>
    My work bridges theory and practice, helping the biopharmaceutical industry transition to smart, connected manufacturing systems.
  </p>
</div>
